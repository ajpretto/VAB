# Cold Call Script — AI Operations for Home Services

**Goal:** Book a 15-minute demo call
**Mindset:** You're not selling. You're finding out if they have a problem you can solve.

---

## OPENER (10 seconds)

> "Hey [Name], this is Anthony. I help [plumbers/HVAC guys/etc] in Tampa stop losing jobs to missed calls. Got 30 seconds?"

**If they say no / bad time:**
> "No problem — when's better, tomorrow morning or afternoon?"

**If they're skeptical:**
> "I'll be quick — just wanted to see if this is even relevant to you."

---

## HOOK (20 seconds)

> "I built an AI system that answers your phone 24/7 — books appointments, qualifies if it's an emergency, texts you a summary. Works while you're on a job site."

**Pause. Let them react.**

---

## PAIN QUESTION (the key moment)

> "Quick question — what happens right now when someone calls and you're under a sink or up on a roof?"

**LISTEN. Let them talk.**

Common responses:
- "Goes to voicemail" → "And how many of those actually call back?"
- "My wife/office handles it" → "What about nights and weekends?"
- "I try to call back" → "How long does that usually take?"
- "I lose some" → "Any idea how many per week?"

---

## AGITATE (make the pain real)

> "Yeah, I hear that a lot. The math is brutal — if you're missing even 5 calls a week at $400-500 per job, that's $8-10K a month walking out the door."

**Pause.**

> "Most of those people aren't leaving voicemails. They're just calling the next guy on Google."

---

## SOLUTION (brief)

> "So what I do is set up an AI receptionist that:
> - Answers every call instantly
> - Books appointments right into your calendar
> - Texts you a summary after each call
> - Works 24/7 — nights, weekends, holidays
> 
> Takes about a week to set up. You don't have to do anything technical."

---

## QUALIFY THEM

> "Can I ask — how many calls do you typically get per day?"

**Listen for:**
- Volume (more calls = more value)
- Current solution (or lack of one)
- Pain level (frustrated = good prospect)

> "And who handles calls now? Is that working well?"

---

## TRIAL CLOSE

> "Here's what I'd like to do — let me set this up for you for free for 2 weeks. You don't pay anything. I just want to prove it works."

> "If it catches even one job you would've missed, you'll see the value. If not, no hard feelings. Fair?"

---

## HANDLE OBJECTIONS

### "How much does it cost?"
> "If we move forward after the trial, it's $497 a month. But honestly, if it doesn't pay for itself in the first week, I'll tell you not to buy it. That's why I do the free trial."

### "I already have someone answering phones"
> "That's great — what happens when they're on another call? Or after hours? This handles the overflow and the nights/weekends."

### "I don't trust AI"
> "I get it. That's exactly why I do the 2-week trial. You'll hear it yourself — most people can't tell it's AI. And if you don't like it, we just turn it off."

### "I'm too busy right now"
> "Totally understand. That's actually the whole point — you're busy ON jobs, so you're missing calls FOR jobs. Let me send you a quick text with my info. When's a better week?"

### "Send me some info"
> "Sure, what's your email? Actually — I'll text you a link right now. What's the best number?"
*(Get their cell, now you have a direct line)*

### "I need to think about it"
> "Totally fair. What specifically do you want to think through? Maybe I can answer it now."

---

## BOOK THE DEMO

> "Cool — let's do a quick 15-minute call where I can show you exactly how it works. I can do [tomorrow at 10] or [Thursday afternoon]. What's better?"

**Always give 2 options, not "when works for you?"**

---

## CLOSE THE CALL

> "Awesome, I've got you down for [day/time]. I'll send you a calendar invite. Looking forward to it, [Name]."

---

## AFTER THE CALL

Send to Gru via voice note:
> "[Company name], talked to [Name], [outcome], [next steps]"

Example:
> "ABC Plumbing, talked to Mike the owner, interested, demo scheduled Thursday 2pm. He's missing about 10 calls a week, wife handles phones but not after 5pm."

---

## KEY PRINCIPLES

1. **Talk less, listen more** — Ask questions, let them sell themselves on the problem
2. **Don't pitch features** — Pitch the outcome (never miss a job)
3. **Use their language** — If they say "leads" use "leads", if they say "calls" use "calls"
4. **Assume the sale** — "When should we do the demo?" not "Would you like a demo?"
5. **Get the cell phone** — Email goes to spam. Text doesn't.
6. **2-week free trial is your weapon** — Eliminates all risk objections

---

**Your one-liner:**
> "I help [trade] businesses stop losing jobs to missed calls using AI."

**The stat:**
> "5 missed calls/week × $500/job = $10,000/month lost"

**The close:**
> "Free 2-week trial. If it doesn't catch at least one job, walk away."
