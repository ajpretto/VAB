## Competitor Strengths to Counter

### ZyraTalk ($99/mo)
- **Their strength:** Cheap, HVAC/plumbing specific
- **Your counter:** No white-glove setup, no local support, you're on your own

### LeadTruffle ($229/mo)
- **Their strength:** Multi-channel (SMS + voice + web), lead aggregator integrations
- **Your counter:** They're a platform, not a partner. No customization, no relationship.

### Avoca AI (Enterprise)
- **Their strength:** Powers big players, ServiceTitan native
- **Your counter:** Built for $100M+ companies. Overkill and overpriced for local operators.

### Smith.ai ($292+/mo)
- **Their strength:** Human backup for complex calls
- **Your counter:** Per-call pricing adds up fast. AI has gotten good enough that humans aren't needed for 95% of calls.
