**"Never Miss Another Call. AI Answers 24/7. White-Glove Setup Included."**

### Target Market
- Plumbers, HVAC contractors, electricians, roofers, pest control
- Tampa Bay area first (Anthony's home turf), then expand
- Sweet spot: 2-10 employees, owner-operated, $500k-$5M revenue
- Pain indicators: bad Google reviews about "no one answered", voicemail-heavy, no after-hours coverage

### The Problem (in their words)
- "I lose jobs because I can't answer the phone while I'm on a ladder"

- "By the time I call back, they've already hired someone else"

### The Solution
AI voice assistant that:
1. **Answers every call instantly** — no hold music, no voicemail
2. **Books appointments directly** into their calendar
3. **Qualifies urgency** — emergency vs. can wait
4. **Collects info** — name, address, problem description
5. **Sends you a text summary** after each call
6. **Works 24/7/365** — weekends, holidays, 2am emergencies

## Sales Approach

### Positioning
You're not selling software. You're selling:
- Peace of mind
- Revenue protection

- Professional image

1. Understand their current pain
2. Quantify the cost of missed calls

Only offer trial if they're hesitant. Lead with full price first.
