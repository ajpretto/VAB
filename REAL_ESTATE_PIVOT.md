## 2. The Problem We Solve

### What Happens Today:

1. Agent is showing a house at 2pm
2. Motivated buyer calls about a $400K listing
3. Call goes to voicemail (if they're lucky)
4. Buyer doesn't leave message — calls next agent on Zillow
5. Agent loses $12,000 commission they never knew existed

### The Pain Points:

- **Can't answer while showing** — Unprofessional to take calls
- **Evenings/weekends are peak** — When people browse Zillow
- **Lead qualification takes time** — Tire-kickers vs. pre-approved buyers
- **Follow-up is inconsistent** — Busy agents forget to call back
- **No one leaves voicemail** — 85% hang up

### The Cost of the Problem:

> "If you miss 5 qualified leads per month, and 1 would have converted, that's **$150K/year** walking out the door."

---

## 3. The Solution

### What We Deliver:

**AI Voice Agent that:**
1. Answers every call instantly, 24/7/365
2. Sounds natural and professional (not robotic IVR)
3. Qualifies leads in real-time:
   - Are they buying or selling?
   - Timeline (now, 3 months, just browsing)?
   - Pre-approved? Budget range?
   - Specific property inquiry or general?
4. Books showing appointments directly into calendar
5. Texts/emails agent with lead summary instantly
6. Handles basic questions (office hours, areas served, process)
7. Transfers hot leads to agent's cell if urgent

### What Makes Us Different:

- **White-glove setup** — We configure everything, agent does nothing technical
- **Real estate specific** — Not generic, trained on real estate conversations
- **Local Tampa focus** — We understand the market
- **Human backup option** — Can transfer to you for hot leads
- **CRM integration** — Syncs with Follow Up Boss, kvCORE, etc.

## 7. The Demo

### Live Demo Flow (15 minutes):

1. **Set context (2 min)**
   - "Let me show you exactly what your callers will experience"
   
2. **They call the demo number (5 min)**
   - Play role of interested buyer
   - Let AI qualify them
   - Book a fake showing
   - See the instant text/email summary

3. **Show the dashboard (3 min)**
   - Call recordings
   - Lead summaries
   - Analytics (calls, qualified leads, appointments)

4. **CRM integration (2 min)**
   - Show how leads auto-sync to Follow Up Boss

5. **Close (3 min)**
   - "What questions do you have?"
   - "Ready to get this set up for your business?"

### Demo Script for AI:

Build a real estate-specific demo agent with:
- Greeting: "Hi, you've reached [Agent Name]'s office. I'm their assistant. Are you calling about buying, selling, or a specific property?"
- Qualification: Timeline, budget, pre-approval, location preferences
- Appointment booking: "I can get you scheduled for a showing. What day works best?"
- Handoff: "Great, I've got you down. [Agent] will reach out within 30 minutes to confirm."
