# Cold Call Tracking System

## How It Works

Anthony sends voice notes to Gru after each call. Gru transcribes, parses, and logs automatically.

### Voice Note Format (flexible)
Just talk naturally:
- "Called [Company], talked to [Name], [outcome], [next steps if any]"
- "ABC Plumbing, owner Mike, interested, demo scheduled Thursday"
- "XYZ HVAC, no answer, will try again tomorrow"
- "Smith Electric, gatekeeper, couldn't get through"

### Outcome Categories
- **INTERESTED** — wants demo/more info
- **DEMO_SCHEDULED** — meeting booked
- **CALLBACK** — asked to call back later
- **NOT_INTERESTED** — clear no
- **NO_ANSWER** — didn't pick up
- **VOICEMAIL** — left message
- **GATEKEEPER** — couldn't reach decision maker
- **WRONG_NUMBER** — bad contact info
