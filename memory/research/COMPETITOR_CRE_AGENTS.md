## Features (40+ Pre-Built Tasks)

### What They Automate:
- Parse rent rolls from raw PDFs → clean data
- Abstract OMs (pull numbers, assumptions, deal points)
- Draft investment summaries for IC committees
- Pull comps (sales, rent, expense) — formatted per firm needs
- Location narratives (demographic, tax, crime, market data)

### Verticals Covered:
- ✅ Acquisitions
- ✅ Asset Management
- ✅ Brokerage
- ✅ Loan Origination
- ✅ Development (mentioned but unclear depth)
- ✅ Portfolio Management

---

## Key Differentiators (Their Claims)

1. **No prompting required** — You assign tasks, they deliver output
2. **Built by CRE veterans** — "not engineers who never prepared a T12"
3. **Institutional-quality output** — IC-ready memos, properly formatted
4. **Integrations planned** — ARGUS, Yardi, etc.

---

## Gap Analysis (Opportunities for Anthony)

### Where CRE Agents Seems Strong:
- Acquisitions workflow (OM abstraction, IC memos)
- Comp pulling and formatting
- Document parsing (rent rolls, PDFs)

### Where Gaps May Exist:
| Gap | Why It Matters |
|-----|----------------|
| **Construction draws** | Real-time tracking, not just underwriting |
| **Budget variance** | Ongoing project management, not deal analysis |
| **Vendor performance** | Historical contractor tracking |
| **RFI/Submittal workflow** | Day-to-day construction ops |
| **Cross-office coordination** | Tampa ↔ Mount Prospect info flow |

### Anthony's Unique Angle:
CRE Agents built from "what CRE pros say they need." Anthony can observe **what they actually do** at Wingspan — the messy reality, not the clean description.

**The insider advantage:** He can find pain points no one is articulating because they've normalized them.

---

## Implications

1. **Don't compete head-on** — $200/mo with 20+ years of CRE expertise behind it. Can't beat that.

2. **Go deeper on development** — They cover development but may not go deep on construction-phase workflows.

3. **Niche is the play** — "AI for RE Development Construction Management" vs. "AI for CRE" (too broad)

4. **Complement, don't replace** — Build something that works alongside CRE Agents, not against it.
