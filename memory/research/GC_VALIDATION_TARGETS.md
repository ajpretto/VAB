## Validation Questions to Ask

When you connect with these GCs, focus on understanding their pain:

### The Pain
1. "How long does it take to put together a monthly draw package?"
2. "What's the most frustrating part of the process?"
3. "How do you chase down sub invoices and lien waivers?"

### The Current Process
4. "What tools do you use to compile draws? (Excel, specialized software, paper?)"
5. "Who in your organization handles this? (Owner, PM, accounting?)"
6. "How many draws are you managing simultaneously?"

### The Opportunity
7. "Have you tried any software to automate this? What worked/didn't work?"
8. "If you could wave a magic wand, what would you change about the process?"
9. "What would saving 5-10 hours per draw per month be worth to you?"
