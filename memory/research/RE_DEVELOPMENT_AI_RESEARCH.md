### Key Pain Points in RE Development (Automation Targets)

From Northspyre research + industry analysis:

**1. Budget Management**
- Pain: Managing budgets in manual spreadsheets = time-consuming, error-prone, siloed
- Problem: Errors require rework, create bottlenecks, derail timelines
- AI Solution: Automated data extraction from project documents, standardized templates, historical benchmarking

**2. Draw Reporting**
- Pain: Construction draw management requires chasing documents, reconciling line items
- Problem: Missing/late draws affect cash flow, contractor relationships
- AI Solution: Automated document processing, compliance checking, timeline tracking

**3. Forecasting**
- Pain: Teams operate on "gut decisions or outdated forecasts"
- Problem: No real-time visibility, operating on stale data
- AI Solution: Real-time portfolio views, predictive analytics, multi-year cash flow projections

**4. Vendor/Contractor Management**
- Pain: No systematic tracking of who bids low, who submits change orders, who delivers
- Problem: Repeat bad contractor decisions
- AI Solution: Vendor analytics, performance scoring, pattern detection

**5. Document Management**
- Pain: RFIs, submittals, meeting minutes, contracts scattered across systems
- Problem: Things fall through cracks, compliance risks
- AI Solution: Agentic systems that draft, organize, and track project documents

**6. Code/Compliance Interpretation**
- Pain: Manual review of codes, zoning requirements, specifications
- Problem: Errors = costly delays, rework
- AI Solution: AI interprets codes and specs to support compliance checks
