## What Each Component Does

### 1. AI Voice Receptionist
- Answers in 1 ring, 24/7/365
- Books appointments directly into your calendar
- Qualifies urgency (emergency vs. can wait)
- Collects: name, address, phone, problem description
- Sends you text summary after each call
- Handles multiple calls at once

### 2. Automated Review System
- Texts customer 2 hours after job completion
- "How was your service today? Tap to leave a review"
- Happy customers → Google review link
- Unhappy customers → Private feedback form (you fix before it's public)
- Increases 5-star reviews by 300%+

### 3. Lead Qualification Bot
- Chat widget on your website
- "What service do you need?" → qualifies job type
- Collects contact info + problem description
- Scores lead quality (A/B/C)
- Hot leads get instant notification to you
- Replaces form fills nobody checks

### 4. Appointment Reminders
- Day-before text: "Reminder: Your appointment tomorrow at 2pm"
- Morning-of call: Confirms they'll be home
- Reschedule option built in
- Reduces no-shows by 50-70%
- Saves drive time to empty houses

### 5. After-Hours Chat Widget
- Website visitors at 11pm still get help
- AI answers common questions
- Captures leads for morning follow-up
- Schedules tentative appointments
- Never lose another late-night emergency
