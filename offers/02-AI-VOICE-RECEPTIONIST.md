## How It Works

**Customer calls → AI answers → Books appointment → Texts you summary**

Example call:
> AI: "Thanks for calling Smith Plumbing, this is Alex. How can I help you today?"
> 
> Caller: "Yeah, my water heater is leaking pretty bad."
> 
> AI: "I'm sorry to hear that. Let me get you scheduled with one of our technicians. Is this an emergency with active flooding, or is it a slow leak we can address within a day or two?"
> 
> Caller: "It's pretty slow, but I'd like someone soon."
> 
> AI: "Absolutely. I have availability tomorrow between 9am and 12pm, or Thursday afternoon. Which works better for you?"
> 
> Caller: "Tomorrow morning works."
> 
> AI: "Perfect. I have you down for tomorrow, March 2nd, between 9am and 12pm. Can I get your name and address?"

**You get a text:**
> 📞 New booking: John Smith, 123 Main St, water heater leak (slow), tomorrow 9am-12pm. Call recording: [link]
