# Automated Review System — $297/month

## Turn Happy Customers Into 5-Star Reviews

### The Problem
- You do great work but have 15 Google reviews
- Competitor has 200 reviews and ranks above you
- Customers forget to leave reviews
- Bad reviews sit unanswered, hurting your reputation

### The Solution
Automatic review requests after every job:
- Customer gets text 2 hours after service
- Happy? → Direct link to Google review
- Unhappy? → Private feedback (you fix it before it goes public)
- Follow-up reminder 3 days later if no response

---

## How It Works

**Job completes → System texts customer → Review or feedback → You win**

**Text to customer (2 hours after job):**
> Hi John! Thanks for choosing Smith Plumbing today. How was your experience? 
> 
> ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ Tap to leave a review: [Google link]
> 
> Had an issue? Reply to this text and we'll make it right.

**If they rate 4-5 stars:** Direct to Google review
**If they rate 1-3 stars:** Routes to private feedback form, you get alert
