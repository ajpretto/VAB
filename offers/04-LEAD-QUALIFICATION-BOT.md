# Lead Qualification Bot — $397/month

## Stop Wasting Time on Bad Leads

### The Problem
- Website form: "I need a quote" — no details
- You call back, play phone tag
- Finally connect: they want a $50 job or live outside your area
- 30 minutes wasted on a lead that was never real

### The Solution
AI chat widget that qualifies BEFORE you call back:
- "What service do you need?"
- "What's your address?" (checks if in service area)
- "Is this an emergency or routine?"
- "What's your budget range?"

**You only call back HOT leads. Everything else is filtered.**

---

## How It Works

**Visitor lands on your site:**

> 💬 "Hi! I'm here to help. What service do you need today?"
> 
> Visitor: "I need my AC looked at"
> 
> 💬 "Got it — AC service. Is this an emergency (no cooling), or routine maintenance?"
> 
> Visitor: "It's not cooling at all, it's 95 degrees"
> 
> 💬 "That sounds urgent. Let me get your info so we can help ASAP. What's your name and address?"

**After qualification, you get:**
> 🔥 HOT LEAD
> Name: Sarah Johnson
> Address: 456 Oak St (in service area ✓)
> Service: AC repair — emergency
> Issue: No cooling, 95°F outside
> Score: A+ 
> Call now: (555) 123-4567

## Lead Scoring

| Score | Criteria | Action |
|-------|----------|--------|
| A+ | Emergency, in area, high-ticket service | Call immediately |
| A | Routine, in area, good service | Call within 2 hours |
| B | In area, small job | Call when available |
| C | Outside area or low intent | Send polite decline |
