# Appointment Reminder System — $197/month

## Eliminate No-Shows, Save Drive Time

### The Problem
- You drive 30 minutes to a job
- Customer isn't home
- "Oh I forgot! Can you come back tomorrow?"
- You lost an hour + gas + the job slot

### The Solution
Automated reminders that CONFIRM attendance:
- Day before: "Your appointment is tomorrow at 2pm. Reply YES to confirm."
- Morning of: Call or text confirming they'll be home
- No response? You get alert before driving out

---

## How It Works

**Day Before (5pm):**
> 📅 Reminder: Your appointment with Smith Plumbing is tomorrow, March 3rd at 2pm.
> 
> Reply YES to confirm, or RESCHEDULE if you need a different time.

**Customer replies YES:** Confirmed, you're good
**Customer replies RESCHEDULE:** AI offers alternative times
**No response:** You get alert, AI sends follow-up

**Morning Of (8am for afternoon appointments):**
> ☀️ Good morning! Just confirming your 2pm appointment today. 
> 
> Our tech will arrive between 2-3pm. Will someone be home? Reply YES or call us to reschedule.
