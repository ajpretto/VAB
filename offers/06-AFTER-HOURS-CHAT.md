## How It Works

**11:47pm visitor on your site:**

> 💬 "Hi! Smith Plumbing is here to help, even after hours. What can I assist you with?"
> 
> Visitor: "Do you guys do water heater installation?"
> 
> 💬 "Yes! We install and repair all types of water heaters — tank and tankless. Are you looking to replace an existing unit or install new?"
> 
> Visitor: "Replace. My current one is 15 years old"
> 
> 💬 "Smart to replace before it fails. I can schedule a free estimate for you. What's a good day this week?"
> 
> Visitor: "Thursday works"
> 
> 💬 "I have Thursday at 10am or 2pm available. Which works better?"

**You get notification:**

> Name: Tom Wilson
> Service: Water heater replacement
> Tentative: Thursday 2pm
> Phone: (555) 987-6543
