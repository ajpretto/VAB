- **Ch 4:** Exponential and Logarithmic Functions (4.1–4.5)
- **Ch 5:** Trigonometric Functions (5.1–5.8)
- **Ch 6:** Analytic Trigonometry (6.1–6.5)
- **Ch 7:** Additional Topics in Trigonometry (7.1–7.5)
- **Ch 8:** Systems of Equations and Inequalities (8.1–8.5)
- **Ch 9:** Matrices and Determinants (9.1–9.5)
- **Ch 10:** Conic Sections (10.1–10.4)

## Study Method

**Each session:**
1. Skim section summaries (5 min)
2. Try mid-chapter exercises without reading (20 min)
3. Read sections where you struggled (30 min)
4. Work harder problems from problem set (25 min)

**If you ace a section:** Skip ahead
**If you struggle:** Slow down, mark for Sunday review
