# Claude Code Prompt: Remotion Video Ad Business Plan

Copy and paste this into your Claude Code session:

---

## Context

I'm a college senior (graduating May 2026) interning at a real estate development company. I created a Remotion video ad for one of our apartment properties and want to turn this into a side business.

## The Business Concept

**What I'm selling:** Short-form video advertisements (15-45 seconds) created with Remotion — programmatic, template-based video generation that produces professional social media ads quickly.

**Target customers:** Any local business that needs compelling social media video ads — restaurants, retail, real estate, services, etc. No specific niche initially.

**Sales approach:** 
1. Create a sample video for a prospect using their publicly available brand assets
2. Send them a teaser (first 10-15 seconds)
3. Offer to deliver the complete video + ongoing service for a fee

**Pricing:** Need help determining — considering either:
- Per-video pricing
- Monthly subscription (unlimited videos)

**MVP goal:** 5-10 paying customers in 90 days

## What I Need You to Build

Create a comprehensive business plan that includes:

### 0. Research: Successful Remotion Videos
**IMPORTANT — Do this first:**
- Search X/Twitter extensively for Remotion video examples that have performed well
- Find creators who are using Remotion for commercial/ad content
- Identify 10-15 video styles/templates that have proven engagement
- Analyze what makes them effective (pacing, transitions, text animation, music sync)
- Document specific Remotion techniques and components used
- Create a "proven templates" reference list I can model my videos after

The Wingspan video I made is just ONE style. I want variety based on what's actually working in the market.

### 1. Business Model & Pricing Strategy
- Research competitive pricing for social media video ads
- Recommend pricing tiers (per-video vs subscription)
- Calculate what I'd need to charge to make this worthwhile alongside school/work

### 2. Remotion Workflow & Templates
- Design a standardized workflow for producing videos efficiently
- Create a template system that can be quickly customized per client
- Define what brand assets I need from clients (logos, colors, fonts, images, copy)
- Build reusable Remotion components I can adapt across industries

### 3. Outreach & Sales System
- Script for cold outreach (email, DM)
- Process for creating teaser videos at scale
- CRM or tracking system for prospects
- Follow-up sequences

### 4. Operations
- Client onboarding checklist (what assets to request)
- Delivery workflow
- Revision policy
- Time estimates per video type

### 5. Legal & Business Setup
- Do I need an LLC?
- Basic contract/terms of service template
- What to include in pricing agreements

### 6. Launch Roadmap
- Key milestones and success metrics (not week-by-week — I find rigid timelines limiting)
- Priority order: what to do first, second, third
- Decision points: when to scale up, pivot, or double down
- How to balance with school (Mon/Wed/Fri internship, graduating May)

## Constraints
- I'm a student with limited time (internship 3 days/week)
- Starting with minimal budget
- Want to prove concept before scaling
- Need systems that let me produce videos quickly once I have brand assets

## Deliverables I Want
1. **Research document:** 10-15 proven Remotion video styles from X with links, analysis, and why they work
2. Written business plan document
3. Remotion project structure with multiple reusable templates (based on research)
4. Outreach scripts and email templates
5. Client onboarding checklist
6. Pricing calculator/spreadsheet
7. Launch roadmap with milestones and priorities (not weekly schedule)

Start by asking me any clarifying questions, then build this out systematically.
