# Nightly Advisory Council

## What This Does
Every night at 10pm EST (3am UTC), a panel of AI "advisors" reviews all business data and delivers ranked recommendations by morning.

## The Council (8 Perspectives)

| Advisor | Role | Focus |
|---------|------|-------|
| **Growth Strategist** | Offense | New opportunities, expansion, scale |
| **Revenue Guardian** | Defense | Protect margins, cut waste, optimize pricing |
| **Skeptical Operator** | Reality check | What could go wrong? What are we missing? |
| **Customer Advocate** | Outside-in | What do customers actually want/need? |
| **Efficiency Expert** | Process | Where are we wasting time/money? |
| **Competitor Analyst** | Market | What are rivals doing? Threats? |
| **Risk Manager** | Downside | Legal, financial, operational risks |
| **Innovation Scout** | Future | New tools, tech, trends to leverage |

## Data Sources Analyzed
- LEADS.md — pipeline health, conversion potential
- TRACKING.md — call outcomes, trends, objections
- OFFER.md — pricing effectiveness
- Competitor intel
- Recent conversations

## Output
Morning Telegram message with:
1. Top 3 priorities for today
2. Key insight from each advisor
3. One "blind spot" we might be missing
4. Recommended actions ranked by impact

- **Runs:** 3:00 AM UTC (10:00 PM EST)
- **Delivers:** By 7:00 AM EST
